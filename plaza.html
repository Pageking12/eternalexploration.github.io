Sharing Cordova Plaza's Green Space

CORDOVAPLAZA

ENVIRONMENTAL

The lively Cordova Plaza is alive with activity, from walks before dawn to celebrations at dusk. People enjoy outings under swaying trees, children chase laughing throughout its wide length. The plaza comes to life with traditional dance performances and cultural activities that highlight the lively personality of Cordova.

Cordova Plaza is a good base of to see all that the island has to offer, and it welcomes guests with open arms. In the area, the lively Roro Port links Cordova with Cebu's mainland, and small cafés and eateries provide an example of the region's cuisine. Local business owners can be seen in the plaza itself, their stands stuffed full of objects and handmade goods.
